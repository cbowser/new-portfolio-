What Should I Wear Today, Lola?

Category

Web Design

form

Weather Website / App

summary

For this assignment, we were required to design and code a weather app. With my family’s pug, Lola, as the center of attention, the weather conditions are indicated by a change in Lola’s outfit. Using playful illustrations and simple animations, this responsive website was intended to be a fun, light-hearted way to brighten even the rainest day.
