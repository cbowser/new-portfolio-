summary

What influences my design process? What inspires my design decisions? Catalog of Influences takes a closer look into the communities that I belong to and the influence they have on me and my work. In this piece, personal and found photographs are arranged alongside various pieces of writing on the topic of community. Color is used to divide the book into three sections — Proximity, Belong, and Exchange — each one a different interpretation of community.
