summary

P.S. Writing Back transforms a collection of letters received from a friend over the course of 18+ years. Written and drawn in various sizes and media, the original items of this collection demonstrate the selflessness of a true friend â€” and the significance of a handmade, handwritten or hand drawn gift. Missing from the original collection is the moment in which each of these items were received. Was the recipient grateful, touched, or unphased? Did she reply often or even at all? Transforming the original pieces into something new, the recipient finally replies.
