For this project, I developed systems based on color, typography and space to ensure that the information was presented in a consistent and clear way. For instance, red and blue were used for the Sackler family and Purdue Pharma, while yellow was used for the victims. Another example would be using italicized type for additional facts and colored text for alternative text such as an email or a drug label.
