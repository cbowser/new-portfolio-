Eating Recovery Center and Pathlight Mood & Anxiety Center

Complete Rebrand, Marketing Collateral, Ad Campaigns, Brand Guidelines

Working with Korak Design, this project involved a complete brand refresh for Eating Recovery Center (ERC) and Pathlight Mood & Anxiety Center (Pathlight). The brand refresh included a total redesign of their websites, brand guidelines, catalogs, marketing collateral, digital ads, and more.

Graphic elements, typography and layout work to tie ERC and Pathlight together, while individual color palettes and content help set them apart as their own individual entities.
